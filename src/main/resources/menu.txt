Hello, this is database console application for school.
You have three tables: students, groups and courses.
You can make some query for it, here are commands to do it:

1 - to find all groups with less or equals student count.
2 - to find all students related to course with given name.
3 - to add new student.
4 - to delete student by ID.
5 - to add student to the specified course by his ID.
6 - to remove student from one of his course by student ID.
e - to close application.
